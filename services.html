Belivers Class & Teaching

The Believers Class is a foundational discipleship programme designed to ground new and existing members in the core truths of the Christian faith. Through structured lessons rooted in scripture, participants are equipped with a solid biblical foundation, understanding of salvation, the Holy Spirit, prayer, and daily Christian living. It is a journey from new birth to maturity in Christ.
